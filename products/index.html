Solvor Paint Products

Manufactured by Solvor General Trading & Investments Company Limited — quality you can trust, durability you can rely on.

Interior

Vinyl Silk Emulsion

A luxury wall finish specially formulated to impart a rich satin-like sheen to interior walls. Keeps walls beautiful for long. Anti-bacterial with ultra-low VOC.

  • Surface: Interior walls & ceilings
  • Finish: Satin / Silk
  • Coverage: ~12 m²/L (2 coats)
  • Dry time: Touch dry 1 hr · Recoat 4 hrs
  • VOC: Ultra-low VOC
  • Dilution: Up to 10% water
Interior

Matt Emulsion

A water-based modified acrylic emulsion paint for interior walls, ceilings, and hard and soft boards. Durable, smooth, with good hiding and spreading power.

  • Surface: Interior walls, ceilings & boards
  • Finish: Matt
  • Coverage: ~10–14 m²/L (2 coats)
  • Dry time: Touch dry 30 min · Recoat 2 hrs
  • VOC: Low VOC
  • Dilution: Up to 15% water
Exterior

Weatherguard Matt

A high-performance, pure acrylic exterior painting system that offers outstanding protection for exterior surfaces. Resists algal attack, dust pickup, and extreme weathering.

  • Surface: Exterior masonry & concrete
  • Finish: Matt
  • Coverage: ~8–10 m²/L (2 coats)
  • Dry time: Touch dry 1 hr · Recoat 4 hrs
  • Feature: Algae & dust resistant, UV-stable
  • Dilution: Up to 10% water
Primer

Undercoat

Suitable for both interior and exterior surfaces. Excellent adhesion to concrete substrate and value for money. A 2-in-1 primer that prepares any surface for top coats.

  • Surface: Interior & exterior masonry
  • Finish: Matt (primer)
  • Coverage: ~10–12 m²/L
  • Dry time: Touch dry 1 hr · Recoat 3 hrs
  • Feature: 2-in-1 primer, seals & bonds
  • Dilution: Up to 20% water
Decorative

Textured Paint

Enables designers to experiment with various styles and aesthetics. Textile and visual wall texture designs add character and enhance the overall ambiance of any space.

  • Surface: Interior & exterior walls
  • Finish: Textured
  • Coverage: ~2–4 m²/L (effect dependent)
  • Dry time: Touch dry 2 hrs · Full cure 24 hrs
  • Feature: Hides surface imperfections
  • Application: Roller, brush or spray
Roofing

Non-Priming Roofing Paint

Synthetic alkyd-based paint suitable for new galvanised iron, aluminium, or asbestos cement sheets. Fast-drying, hard wearing, flexible and light-fast.

  • Surface: Galvanised iron, aluminium, asbestos
  • Finish: Hard gloss
  • Coverage: ~10–12 m²/L
  • Dry time: Touch dry 1 hr · Recoat 6 hrs
  • Feature: Chemical & UV resistant
  • Dilution: Up to 10% mineral spirits
Additive

Binder

A nontoxic, zero VOC copolymer formula that increases adhesion in water-based paints. Great for lime washes, cellulose distemper paints, and milk paints.

  • Use: Additive for water-based paints
  • VOC: Zero VOC · Non-toxic
  • Dilution ratio: 1 part binder : 3–5 parts paint
  • Feature: Improves adhesion & durability
  • Feature: Extends paint life
  • Application: Mix into paint before use
