# Solvor General Trading & Investments Company Limited

Official website for **Solvor General Trading & Investments Company Limited** — a legally registered company in South Sudan dealing in paints, welding, doors, glass, and general construction across East Africa.

## Pages

| File | Page |
|---|---|
| `index.html` | Home — hero carousel, featured colours, services overview |
| `about.html` | About — company intro, mission, vision, values |
| `services.html` | Services — painting, welding, doors, glass, construction |
| `products.html` | Products — full Solvor Paints product range |

| `contact.html` | Contact — enquiry form and company details |
