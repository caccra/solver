Solvor Paint Products

Manufactured by Solvor General Trading & Investments Company Limited — quality you can trust, durability you can rely on.

Vinyl Silk Emulsion

A luxury wall finish specially formulated to impart a rich satin-like sheen to interior walls. Keeps walls beautiful for long. Anti-bacterial with ultra-low VOC.

  • Use: Interior walls
  • Finish: Satin / Silk
  • Feature: Anti-bacterial, Ultra-low VOC

Matt Emulsion

A water-based modified acrylic emulsion paint for interior walls, ceilings, and hard and soft boards. Durable, smooth, with good hiding and spreading power.

  • Use: Interior walls, ceilings & boards
  • Finish: Matt
  • Feature: Durable, smooth, good coverage

Weatherguard Matt

A high-performance, pure acrylic exterior painting system that offers outstanding protection for exterior surfaces. Resists algal attack, dust pickup, and extreme weathering.

  • Use: Exterior surfaces
  • Finish: Matt
  • Feature: Algae & dust resistant, weatherproof

Undercoat

Suitable for both interior and exterior surfaces. Excellent adhesion to concrete substrate and value for money. A 2-in-1 primer that prepares any surface for top coats.

  • Use: Interior & exterior
  • Finish: Matt (primer)
  • Feature: 2-in-1 primer, concrete adhesion

Textured Paint

Enables designers to experiment with various styles and aesthetics. Both textile and visual wall texture designs play essential roles in interior design, adding character and enhancing the overall ambiance of a space.

  • Use: Interior & exterior walls
  • Finish: Textured
  • Feature: Decorative, character-enhancing

Non-Priming Roofing Paint

Synthetic alkyd-based paint suitable for new galvanised iron, aluminium, or asbestos cement sheets. Fast-drying, hard wearing, flexible and light-fast. Resistant to oils, fats, and common acids and alkalis.

  • Use: Roofing — galvanised, aluminium, asbestos
  • Finish: Hard gloss
  • Feature: Fast-drying, chemical resistant

Binder

A nontoxic, zero VOC copolymer formula that increases adhesion in water-based paints. Great for lime washes, cellulose distemper paints, and milk paints.

  • Use: Additive for water-based paints
  • Feature: Nontoxic, zero VOC
  • Feature: Improves adhesion & durability
