Proudly South Sudanese — Built Here, For Here

Solvor Paints is more than a paint company. We are a South Sudanese brand, born and built in Juba, invested in the future of this nation.

Every bucket of paint we manufacture, every building we colour, every door we weld — it is a direct contribution to the growth and development of South Sudan. We are proud to be part of the story of this great nation as it builds, grows and rises.

From the apartments of Gudele to offices across Juba, from Renk to Warrap — when you see a freshly painted building in South Sudan, there is a good chance Solvor was part of it.
